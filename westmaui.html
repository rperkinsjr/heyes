West Maui,
Iao Valley,
Kahakuloa Details

We tour historic Lāhaina town, past the scenic west shores of Kā‘anapali and Kāhana on to our first (of many) picture taking opportunities, Honolua Bay. Soon thereafter, we will arrive at the ‘blow hole’ for an optional short five minute walk to enjoy this natural occurrence.

The van tour continues through West Maui’s rain forests along a costal scenic route until we reach Kahakuloa Village for a rest stop & shopping opportunity. Kaukini Gallery hosts 156 local artist’s work with wonderful unique craftsmanship.

Iao Valley features a leisurely walk around a state park filled with Native Hawaiian flowers, plants, flowing streams and unforgettable valley walls. The tour guides will give insight into lā‘au lapa‘au (Hawaiian medicinal plants and herbs).

Our last stop will be Maui Tropical Plantation. A brief shopping time in the tropical market, as well as viewing of well maintained plants and various fruit trees, is a pleasant stop.
