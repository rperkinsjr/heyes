Aloha Spirit.
That is what it is all about!

Hawaiian Eyes Alternative Tours and Flight Line Services is a company created with my knowledge of what the "Aloha Spirit" is truly all about and my abiding love for this land, it's people and unique culture.

There's so much to see and do on Maui while still maintaining it's appeal as a tranquil island oasis. Maui caters to those who desire rest and relaxation as well as the avid adventure seeker who wants to do and see everything. Hawaiian Eyes Tours was created understanding the need for both peace and excitement and the popular tours shown here satisfy each. We also have the flexibility to customize tours to individual parties which offers endless possibilities for a memorable Maui experience.

For instance, the lush Hana rain forest is a tour favorite with it's gorgeous waterfalls and tropical vegetation. The infamous road to Hana is also an adventure in itself with breathtaking scenery winding along the dramatic cliffs of the Maui coastline. Or maybe taking a leisurely ride through the Maui countryside to a wonderful winery for a taste of tropical Maui wines is more your pace.

From majestic Hana waterfalls to the world class shops and galleries on Lahaina's famed Front Street and more spectacular Maui sunsets than you can imagine, Hawaiian Eyes will show you the best Maui has to offer. Whether you are newlyweds, celebrating your 25 wedding anniversary, vacationing with your family or regular visitors of the beautiful Hawaiian Islands, you will never forget your time spent with us on Magical Maui! So please...come and see Hawaii through my eyes...Hawaiian Eyes

Mahalo Nui Loa (Thank you very much),
